About Us

Welcome to RiverScout – your intelligent river health monitoring companion.

RiverScout is a real-time river monitoring system that not only collects essential water quality data through a physical prototype but also empowers users with intelligent insights and visualizations.

Our hardware prototype is equipped with environmental sensors that measure turbidity, pH, total dissolved solids (TDS), water and ambient temperature, and environmental humidity. This data is sent to the RiverScout platform for advanced analysis.

Key Features

  • 📡 Real-Time Data Collection from rivers using a working physical prototype
  • 📊 Graphical Analysis for trends and patterns
  • 🗺️ Map Visualizations to understand geographical data impact
  • 🧠 GenAI-Based Responses that interpret data and offer actionable insights
  • 🌐 Interactive Dashboard for easy data access and monitoring

Our Vision

We aim to revolutionize how environmental data is collected — making river monitoring smarter, accessible, and actionable. By integrating IoT with AI, we support government bodies, NGOs, and communities in making data-driven decisions for water resource protection. We envision a future where real-time insights drive policy and conservation efforts, enabling faster response to pollution and environmental threats. Through advanced analytics and intuitive dashboards, we empower stakeholders to monitor trends, predict risks, and take meaningful action. RiverScout is committed to bridging the gap between raw environmental data and informed ecological stewardship.
